  TAMUhack

  github.com/EshwarReddy13/Buckle-UP

- Developed an app that would track the driver's location, speed, acceleration and deceleration and compare it to the speed limits and traffic rules set by the government. Based on this comparison, the driver's behavior is analyzed and points are given.

- In addition to the rewards system, the app also provides regular performance reports to the driver that help them track their driving behavior and improvements over time.

The app also has a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for drivers to understand their driving behavior, track their progress and redeem their rewards.

  Chad-bot/AI-bot

  github.com/EshwarReddy13/ChadBot

- Developed an AI bot using Python and Pygame. Its main goal was to find the shortest path out of the maze. In order to achieve this, the A* path searching algorithm was utilized. This allowed the bot to find the shortest way through the maze while also avoiding the enemy that kept randomly moving around.

- To further improve the accuracy and efficiency of the bot, a heuristic function was developed. This function calculated the heuristic value of each node, taking into account factors such as the distance to the end goal.

- By combining the A* search algorithm with the heuristic function, we were able to find the shortest path through the maze, ensuring that the bot reached the end goal in the fastest and most efficient manner possible. The implementation of the heuristic function greatly improved the performance of the AI bot.

  TamuDatathon

devpost.com/software/celonis-2021-challenge-14pkv9

- The data we collected from various sources such as customer reviews, sales data, and delivery information was processed using Celonis software and the pandas library. The software helped us to identify the inefficiencies in the pizza business process, including bottlenecks in the supply chain and customer complaints.

- By utilizing machine learning algorithms, we were able to find patterns in the data, which led us to make data-driven recommendations to the business owner. One of the key areas we found was a lack of communication between the front-end customer service team and the back-end kitchen team, which was causing delays in order processing.

- We suggested the implementation of an automated system to monitor and streamline the communication between the two teams, which significantly reduced order processing times. As a result, the customer satisfaction levels increased, leading to an increase in sales and overall profits for the business.
